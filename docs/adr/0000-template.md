# NNNN. Short title of the decision

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the problem or force that requires a decision? Describe the
situation, constraints and options considered. Keep it factual — this is
the "why", not the "what".

## Decision

The decision that was made, stated in the active voice: "We will ...".

## Consequences

What becomes easier or harder as a result. Include both the positive
outcomes and the trade-offs or follow-up work the decision creates.
